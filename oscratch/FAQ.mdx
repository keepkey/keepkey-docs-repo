# KeepKey FAQ

## How To

1. **How do I recover my device from seed?**
Check out our [recovery guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

2. **How do I send Bitcoin with my KeepKey?**
Refer to our [Sending Bitcoin guide](https://medium.com/keepkeydevs/how-to-send-and-receive-bitcoin-in-keepkey-desktop-858e70312611).

3. **How do I update my firmware?**
Follow our [Updating your KeepKey guide](https://medium.com/@highlander_35968/updating-your-keepkey-154a49445d4c).

4. **How do I use the Pin cipher?**
See our guide on [Understanding the KeepKey PIN](https://medium.com/@highlander_35968/how-to-wipe-your-keepkey-88c8aa227950).

5. **How do I wipe my KeepKey?**
Check our [How to wipe your KeepKey guide](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/wipe).

6. **Can I use a KeepKey with my Phone?**
No, KeepKey requires a USB connection to KeepKey Desktop and is only compatible with Windows/MacOS/Linux.

7. **What Coins can I use with KeepKey?**
View our [coin support page](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery).

8. **I can't find XRP/DASH/DGB in my dashboard. How do I use my Ripple?**
ShapeShift has removed support for these assets. You can find our new [standalone applications](https://keepkey-docs-o9qn.vercel.app/Basics/Recovery) for managing these assets.

## General

1. **Is KeepKey Open Source?**
Yes! KeepKey firmware has a deterministic build process. The community can audit every release we have made over the last 9 years. More information: [Deterministic Build process](https://medium.com/@highlander_35968/deterministic-build-process-11674787d92f).

2. **How do I buy crypto?**
ShapeShift makes it simple to buy/sell crypto and is integrated natively inside KeepKey Desktop! Read more about [ShapeShift here](https://shapeshift.com).

3. **Can I trade and swap crypto?**
Yes! Our native Thorchain integration allows frictionless native swapping without using wrapped tokens on bridges. Read more [here](https://thorchain.com).

4. **Is KeepKey owned by ShapeShift DAO?**
No, KeepKey is owned and operated by former employees of ShapeShift independently. KeepKey continues to fund the ShapeShift DAO. [KeepKey Workstream](https://snapshot.org/#/shapeshiftdao.eth/proposal/0x2528af775ea702a12da168b6d7f8438ba4eae1cebf43d73b36ad1ddb3edef260).

5. **How do I use ShapeShift with my KeepKey?**
ShapeShift is listed in our dapp store. [How to open ShapeShift](https://medium.com/@highlander_35968/how-to-open-shapeshift-dapp-from-inside-keepkey-desktop-310bb16cc7c).

## Troubleshooting

1. **My KeepKey is not recognized by my computer**
Check out our [troubleshooting guide](https://medium.com/@highlander_35968/troubleshooting-connections-with-the-keepkey-4599f1aaee0).

2. **I can't get ShapeShift.com or KeepKey Desktop to recognize my device, what do I do?**
You can use KKcli to update your firmware. [Using KKcli to debug your KeepKey](https://medium.com/@highlander_35968/using-kkcli-to-debug-your-keepkey-3830b504edff).

3. **What do I do if I lose my PIN?**
Wipe your device and proceed with recovery.

4. **What do I do if I lose my KeepKey?**
Purchase a new KeepKey and proceed with recovery.

5. **What do I do if I lose my Recovery Phrase?**
Purchase a new KeepKey. Initialize and set up the new device. Transfer your funds to the new device.
