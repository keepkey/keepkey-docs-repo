# Connecting to DApps with KeepKey Using WalletConnect

KeepKey now offers seamless integration with decentralized applications (DApps) through WalletConnect, enhancing your ability to interact with the world of decentralized finance (DeFi) while maintaining the security of your hardware wallet.

## What is WalletConnect?

WalletConnect is an open-source protocol that enables secure communication between wallets and DApps. It allows you to connect your KeepKey hardware wallet to various DApps without exposing your private keys.

## How to Use WalletConnect with KeepKey

1. **Open a DApp**: Start by opening a DApp in the KeepKey Desktop client's built-in browser.

2. **Select WalletConnect**: Look for the "Connect Wallet" or similar option in the DApp, and choose "WalletConnect".

3. **Copy Connection String**: The DApp will display a WalletConnect QR code. Click "Copy to Clipboard" to copy the connection string.

4. **Connect via KeepKey Desktop**: In the KeepKey Desktop app, click the WalletConnect icon in the top right corner.

5. **Paste and Connect**: Paste the connection string into the provided field and confirm the connection.

6. **Verify Connection**: Check that the DApp icon in your WalletConnect session window matches the one you're connecting to.

## Benefits of Using WalletConnect with KeepKey

- **Enhanced Security**: Your private keys never leave your KeepKey device.
- **Wide Compatibility**: Access a vast ecosystem of DApps.
- **User-Friendly**: Simplifies the process of connecting to DApps.
- **Seamless Integration**: Works smoothly with KeepKey's existing features.

By leveraging WalletConnect, KeepKey users can now explore and interact with a broader range of DeFi opportunities while maintaining the robust security of their hardware wallet.
